Prototype Overview

We are currently using a Things Uno device connected to a DHT22 sensor to gather temperature and humidity. The Things Uno was chosen for its simplicity and documentation. The DHT22 was chosen as an initial sensor because of its ease of use, reliability, and because temperature and humidity are easily understood measurements.

The current power source for the prototype devices is a AA battery pack. Despite our best efforts of reducing power draw, we only managed to achieve data transmission for two weeks from alkaline AA batteries. However, on future devices, there will likely be a more power efficient Arduino device used, which would increase the battery life of the device significantly.
